I Once Knew

I once know someone who cared for me, who loved me, who made me happy, who saved me…who killed me...

Who are you?

Out of everything that happened, this felt even better. It could have been better...

Suddenly, we heard a gunshot and at that moment we both stiffened and the place seem so quite. I smell blood, it was filling up my nose and that was the worst of all that happened. He was still in my arms and said words that killed me more than a gunshot.

“Please….wait…for…me…even…if…I…can’t…”

It wasn’t me, it wasn’t mine…the blood...it was his.

The light was so bright for anyone and anything. Now, it is all empty and the space is covered in darkness. But what did I do? Did I sleep? Or did I wake up?
