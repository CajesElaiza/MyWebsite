I Once Remember

I once remember those undefined memories. I can’t see anything just two people with eyes full of wonder and someone..... someone who filled me tragedy of emotions...

I immediately ran out of the gallery. I did not dare to take a much longer look to the portrait; it was just that if I do I couldn’t hold back my emotions anymore. I ran out of the house as far as I can, memories from that strange happening appeared and for the first time I felt it repeated at that moment; I was running and crying at the same time. I stopped at the village playground. There were only a few people around the place. I sat under a tree and without any waste of time I cried in that place for a long time.

It was almost dark but I’m still in the village playground. I’m sitting on the bench near the swing. My eyes and nose were swollen from crying and no matter how I try to stop the tears from falling down my eyes it still continues. My head feels numb and my chest hurts that I can hardly breathe. I feel so empty at the moment, empty that I can’t even understand. I’ve been feeling strange lately and sometimes I can’t hold back anymore. I let out every tear I’ve been trying to keep when I cried earlier. But it hasn’t stop as if it was endless. There is nothing much that I want now than to stop this terrible misery.

Why is this happening to me anyway?

I closed my eyes but every time I do that I see myself with that man. In that image, we were laughing and we both look so happy together. It feels like I was remembering something but I wasn’t able to recognize it. I don’t understand everything even myself. As I open my eyes back, my heart aches more and more that I don’t even know what am I going to do.

Which choice was I heading? Am I sleeping everything that’s happening now is just a dream? Did I wake up to face this reality? Or am I heading to a much more unexpecting path?

Who are you?
