I Once Knew

I once know someone who cared for me, who loved me, who made me happy, who saved me…who killed me...

Where am I? Why am I running? Why am I scared? Why are those people following me? What is happening?

Then I stopped, still it wasn’t me. I am nothing here but a pain in my chest was now burning and I can’t even understand why such pain is here. It was my body; when I looked at the reflection in the mirror earlier, it was definitely me. I looked different and everything feels wrong. Then again, my consciousness was stunned and confused. Only my heart and body seems to know what they are doing and what I’m going to do.

I looked ahead and saw a man. A man wearing a black coat, tall, handsome and masculine was looking at me. My consciousness described him as that but a much painful agony started burning and hurting in my chest. The fear was gone and sadness overtook everything…so much sadness that it feels more than a torture.

He was now in front of me, his eyes were shining and it was longing. He took me in his arms and as if on cue I burst out crying. He was hugging me so tight not wanting to let me go. I felt him crying and his tears were flowing down my shoulder. There were too much tears…..too much that I can’t even figure out the reason. All I knew a while ago I was running to escape but now it felt like I was actually both trying to escape and looking for this man. Still, this wasn’t me. My body and heart were still in control.
