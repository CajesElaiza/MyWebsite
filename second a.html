I Once Remember

I once remember those undefined memories. I can’t see anything just two people with eyes full of wonder and someone..... someone who filled me tragedy of emotions...

Later on, my best friend grabbed towards a portrait placed in a more secluded area of the gallery. She kept on talking about the portrait however, this time; I completely lost focus to her. My eyes were glued to the portrait and that torturing pain from past few days all came back unexpectedly.

“Isn’t he handsome?” My best friend asked.

He was wearing a tuxedo and he looks calm and positive; he looks quite different but his eyes were still the same. They were shining and longing. I took a little closer step towards the portrait. I just want to make myself sure if...it’s really him. That man...could he be...
