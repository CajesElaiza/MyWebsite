I Forget a Thousand Times

I once know but I keep on forgetting. I once remember but I keep on forgetting. At the end, I always forget or...did I just choose to forget? A thousand times? Forget that I know and forget that I remember...

It was all so sudden. Everything was all so sudden. I once knew him, I once remember him but I'm stopping myself from grasping it all and never certain where it'll all end. I was scared, scared that this could all be a dream and that I've never loved someone like him. We never had memories. I was scared because this could all be reality and that I have to face those memories once again without even knowing they were actually real…that I have to end up being alone.

However, fate changed and I hope we can now find our happiness…together.

They say oblivion is the almighty’s grace to our soul. However, we had the choices. I might have longed to know and remember for a thousand times…prayed for a thousand times but still forgets. It could be that I’m meant to receive it or I did choose oblivion…I chose to forget. For the reason that perhaps, I want destiny to be on my side without me knowing anything…everything. I chose it so that even though I forget the memories, I would still want to be with him without me remembering, it is what I’m longing for.

If I could still have the choices once again in this life, it would still be the same. I would choose to forget again even for a thousand times until destiny will finally bring us together for a thousand times.
