I Once Remember

I once remember those undefined memories. I can’t see anything just two people with eyes full of wonder and someone..... someone who filled me tragedy of emotions...

Who are you? It is the question which kept on running around my mind. I never stopped thinking about what happened whether it was a delusion or it was a reality.

I am in my best friend’s house to compose myself from too much thinking. For the past few days, I wasn’t totally in myself every time I remember that strange happening. I just woke up crying from asleep the next morning. It still left me confuse and the feeling as if it did happen. I can’t totally recall everything but…that man…I just can’t keep him out from my mind.

I heard my best friend calling me from their gallery. Collecting artworks and paintings are my best friend’s favorite hobby. She just collects and talks about them but she never had much talent when it comes to arts. I totally found it rather perplexing.It was quite fortunate for them to be able to provide a gallery in their own house. It was not a problem given that her family also praises artistry.

As I step inside the gallery, I felt the lovely ambiance of the room. It looks so lively and wonderful from the sight of different artworks. It was always the kind of feeling that I usually get whenever I come in here. From the looks of it, they had new displays and the recent ones were kept nicely in a room.I kept on walking around looking from each and one other works not minding much of what my best friend was telling. The paintings look incredibly beautiful. Sometimes I stopped for a few minutes to paintings which I found attractive to my eyes.
