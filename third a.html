I Forget a Thousand Times

I once know but I keep on forgetting. I once remember but I keep on forgetting. At the end, I always forget or...did I just choose to forget? A thousand times? Forget that I know and forget that I remember...

It’s him. That man…he’s here…the portrait... it’s him...

The man who I saw many days ago not knowing if any of those is just a dream or reality. The man whose bothering me and making me feel strange burst of emotions I’ve been keeping since that day. He was the one who made me miserable for the past days. Here I am, standing a few meters in front of his house.

It was sooner than later that he saw me and looked at me for a long time. We are both left frozen from where we are standing at the moment. The look in his eyes never changed; they were shining and longing at the same time. They were strangely beautiful. Our eyes were locked and again the emotions started flowing simultaneously. I can’t stop it. It was too much.

I hurriedly got in the car and drove as far as I can. I didn’t even dare to look back…not even a glance. It terribly hurts inside and every second I feel I’m getting farther…unexplainably, the pain gets worse. The tears just won’t stop falling like a heavy rain and it’s hard to breathe at the moment, it’s killing me. This torture is killing me. When is this going to end? How?

*Screeeeeech! Boogsh!*

Blood…I smell blood. All over the place…all over me…will I end up being alone once again?

“Please….wait…for…me…even…if…I…can’t…”

His voice keeps echoing in my head. People may tell me I’ve gone crazy…but I’m not. I heard him because he’s here. He followed me all the way through here. His face was bleeding but his eyes were looking at me. He was looking at me like he wanted to see me for a long time and now he finally did.
